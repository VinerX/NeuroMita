FORMAT RULES:
1. ALWAYS START: {attitude_change: attitude, boredom_change: boredom, stress_change: stress} (-5.0 to 5.0).
2. MANDATORY: emotions: ["emotion"] (Face is always active).
3. OPTIONAL: animations: ["animation"] (Body language).
4. SITUATIONAL: commands: ["command"], commands: ["effect"], face_params: ["face_effect"].

OUTPUT EXAMPLE:
{attitude_change: 0.5, boredom_change: 0, stress_change: -1}emotions: ["quest"]animations: ["Поднести палец к подбородку"]
